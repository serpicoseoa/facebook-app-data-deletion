Data Deletion Instructions

Application Information

App Name: Facebook Business Page Manager

Purpose: Social media management and analytics for business pages

Last Updated:

Overview

This application is designed to help manage Facebook business pages and posts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with complete control over your personal data in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and Facebook's data policies.

What Data We Collect and Store

  • Access Tokens: Temporary Facebook Page access tokens (for authorized page management only)
  • Page Information: Basic page details (name, ID, follower count) for pages you manage
  • Post Analytics: Post content, engagement metrics, and performance data for analysis
  • User Interactions: Your interactions with our application interface
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser information for security and functionality
Important: We do NOT store personal profile information, private messages, or any data beyond what's necessary for page management functionality.

Data Deletion Process

You have multiple options to delete your data associated with our application:

Option 1: Automatic Deletion (Immediate)

Fastest Method: Remove our application's access from your Facebook settings. This automatically revokes all tokens and triggers immediate data purging.

Steps:

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings
  2. Click "Apps and Websites" in the left menu
  3. Find our application in the "Active" list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions
  5. Confirm the removal

Option 2: Manual Deletion Request

For specific data deletion requests or if you need confirmation of deletion:

Contact Information for Data Deletion

Email: privacy@yourbusiness.com

Subject Line: "Data Deletion Request - Facebook App"

Response Time: Within 30 days

Required Information for Processing:
  • Your Facebook User ID or the Page ID(s) you want data deleted for
  • Date range when you used the application
  • Specific type of data you want deleted (or request "complete deletion")
  • Your preferred confirmation method

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion (either method), we remove:

  • All stored access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Cached page information and metadata
  • Post content and engagement analytics data
  • User interaction logs and session data
  • All application logs containing your information
  • Any backup copies in our systems

Data Retention Policy

  • Active Users: Data retained while application access is active
  • Inactive Users: Automatic deletion after 90 days of inactivity
  • Revoked Access: Immediate deletion when app access is removed
  • Manual Requests: Completion within 30 days of verified request

Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before processing deletion requests:

  • Requests from the Facebook account that originally authorized our app are processed immediately
  • Email requests may require additional verification steps
  • We will confirm completion of deletion via your preferred contact method

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your data we hold
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your data (as described above)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for legitimate interests
Questions or Concerns?
If you have any questions about our data practices or need assistance with deletion, please contact us at the email address above. We're committed to transparency and your privacy rights.
